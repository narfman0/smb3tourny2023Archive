narfman0 03/07/2023 12:11 AM
The group stage has begun! This will be groups of 5 -> 2, which means there are a ton of group stage matches. They can be played out of order, so I'd suggest batching some races back-to-back. We will favor match Win:Loss, but if needed, we will use total run time across the group stage. e.g. if bob is 2-2 and had two 55 minute runs, and alice is 2-2 and had two 54 minute runs, alice would proceed over bob. We have 35 human players, and -> 2 means 7 groups will result in only 14 finals participants. To bring it up to 16, we will pick two players that have the lowest time summed up across all matches. Apologies for the complexity, 35 is a weird number to group how we wanted it, but I believe in us and glhf :)
